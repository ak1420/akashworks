Frequently asked  questions

What does PlutoX specialize in?

PlutoX specializes in crafting high-quality clothing, including t-shirts, shirts, and denims, meticulously designed for modern fashion enthusiasts seeking distinctive style and comfort.

How does PlutoX ensure product quality?

Our skilled artisans employ meticulous craftsmanship, and we source only the finest materials to create products that exemplify durability and comfort. Each piece undergoes rigorous quality checks to meet our high standards.

How do I determine my size when purchasing online?

Our comprehensive size guide provides accurate measurements to help you select the perfect fit. Additionally, our customer service team is ready to assist with any sizing queries.

Yes, sustainability is paramount to us. We prioritize eco-friendly practices in our manufacturing processes, including responsible material sourcing and ethical production methods.

Absolutely! PlutoX offers custom manufacturing solutions, allowing you to tailor designs, fabrics, and styles to suit your brand's unique identity and requirements.

Does PlutoX offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping to bring our exceptional products to customers across the globe. Shipping times and costs may vary depending on the destination.
